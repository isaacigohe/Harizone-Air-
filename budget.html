💡 Money-Saving Tips

Smart ways to stretch your travel budget further.

✈️ Book Flights Early

Flights booked 6–8 weeks in advance are often 20–40% cheaper. Use flight comparison tools.

🏨 Travel Off-Peak

Visit popular destinations in shoulder seasons (April-May, Sep-Oct) for lower costs and fewer crowds.

🍜 Eat Like a Local

Skip tourist restaurants and eat where locals eat — usually better food at a fraction of the price.

🎫 Get City Passes

Many cities offer tourist passes with bundled attractions and transport — great value for sightseers.
