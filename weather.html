Weather Tips for Travelers

Knowing the weather can save your trip!

Rainy Seasons

Southeast Asia's monsoon season runs June–September. Pack light rain gear and plan indoor activities as backup.

Summer Travel

Mediterranean summers are hot and dry. Visit beaches early morning to avoid peak heat. Stay hydrated!

Winter Escapes

Escape winter by heading to East Africa, the Caribbean, or Southeast Asia for warm sunny weather all December.
